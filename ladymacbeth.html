Lady Macbeth

Lady Macbeth, is the wife of Macbeth. With Macbeth, she has growing ambition to have power and position. Lady Macbeth has control over the relationship and when Macbeth does not want to do somwething bad, she manipulates him into doing it. However, Lady Macbeth is filled with guilt she ends up commiting suicide, leaving her husband in his own.
